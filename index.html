How it Works: Sending

  1. Visit your nearest collection point.
  2. Provide ID, phone number, address, and recipient details.
  3. Pay for your package according to size and delivery speed.
  4. Receive a tracking reference number by SMS.
  5. Track updates via website, WhatsApp channel, or SMS.

How it Works: Receiving

  1. Visit your nearest collection point.
  2. Provide reference number and ID number.
  3. Collect your package.
  4. Use SMS or WhatsApp channel for status updates.
